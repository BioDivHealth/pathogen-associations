yellow fever	Aedes taeniorhynchus	mosquito	candidate	field	coastal lowlands, southeastern Brazil	Abreu et al. 2019	first natural infection; local role
```

Extraction notes
- Broad outbreak field study from southeastern Brazil; partial support only, not a complete yellow fever vector list.
- `Sabethes chloropterus`, `Aedes scapularis`, and `Aedes taeniorhynchus` are kept conservative because the paper frames them as local, secondary, or first-detection roles.
- `Aedes aegypti` and `Aedes albopictus` were discussed as negative/no-evidence findings and were omitted from rows.
