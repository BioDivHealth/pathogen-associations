- The strongest non-primary rows are `Aedes japonicus`, `Aedes detritus`, and `Aedes vexans` at higher temperatures.

## Delrieu et al. - 2023 - Temperature and transmission of chikungunya, dengue, and Zika viruses A systematic review of experi.pdf

Extraction notes

- Broad systematic review, but for Zika it is effectively limited to `Aedes aegypti` and `Aedes albopictus`.

- `Aedes albopictus` is the weaker row because the review emphasizes mixed experimental findings.
