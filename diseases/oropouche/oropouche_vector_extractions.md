```tsv
oropouche fever	Aedes serratus	mosquito	candidate	review		González et al. 2025	potential vector
```

Extraction notes

- Narrow outbreak-focused field report from Cuba; partial support only, not a complete species list.
- `Culex quinquefasciatus` and `Aedes aegypti` are kept conservative because this paper provides field RNA detections, not competence confirmation.
- Family-level `Ceratopogonidae` positives were omitted because the source did not identify them to species.

## Sakkas et al. - 2018 - Oropouche Fever A Review.pdf

```tsv
oropouche fever	Culicoides paraensis	midge	confirmed	review		Sakkas et al. 2018	primary urban vector
oropouche fever	Coquillettidia venezuelensis	mosquito	probable	review		Sakkas et al. 2018	secondary vector; sylvatic cycle
oropouche fever	Aedes (Ochlerotatus) serratus	mosquito	probable	review		Sakkas et al. 2018	secondary vector; sylvatic cycle
oropouche fever	Culex quinquefasciatus	mosquito	candidate	review		Sakkas et al. 2018	less important vector
oropouche fever	Culicoides	midge	candidate	review		Sakkas et al. 2018	genus-level sylvatic vector
```

Extraction notes

- Broad review; partial support only, not a complete species list.
- `Coquillettidia venezuelensis` and `Aedes (Ochlerotatus) serratus` are stronger here than in some other reviews because this paper explicitly calls them secondary vectors.
- `Culex quinquefasciatus` and genus-level `Culicoides` remain the most uncertain rows.
