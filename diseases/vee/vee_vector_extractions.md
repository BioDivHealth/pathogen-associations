## Forrester et al. - 2012 - Vector-Borne Transmission Imposes a Severe Bottleneck on an RNA Virus Population.pdf

```csv
Venezuelan equine encephalitis virus,Culex (Melanoconion) taeniopus,mosquito,confirmed,lab,,Forrester et al. 2012,natural enzootic vector
Venezuelan equine encephalitis virus,Aedes (Ochlerotatus) taeniorhynchus,mosquito,probable,review,,Forrester et al. 2012,epizootic vector; prior study cited
```

Extraction notes

- Narrow experimental bottleneck paper; partial support only, not a complete species list.
- `Culex (Melanoconion) taeniopus` is the core row because the paper directly studies VEEV infection and transmission dynamics in this mosquito.
- `Aedes (Ochlerotatus) taeniorhynchus` is more indirect and is retained conservatively from a cited comparison with the epizootic vector.
