## Hu et al. - 2020 - Role of three tick species in the maintenance and transmission of Severe Fever with Thrombocytopenia.pdf

```tsv
SFTSV	Haemaphysalis longicornis	tick	confirmed	field+lab	China	Hu et al. 2020	positive control; established vector
SFTSV	Ixodes sinensis	tick	confirmed	lab	China	Hu et al. 2020	efficient vector; transovarial

SFTSV	Ixodes persulcatus	tick	weak	lab	China	Hu et al. 2020	PCR only; no transmission
```

Extraction notes

- Narrow experimental study from China; partial support only, not a complete SFTSV tick list.
- `Haemaphysalis longicornis` and `Ixodes sinensis` are the strongest rows.
- `Dermacentor silvarum` and `Ixodes persulcatus` are weak because the paper does not demonstrate efficient transmission.
